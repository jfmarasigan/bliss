Introduction

What is CTPL?

Compulsory Third Party Liability

Compulsory Third Party Liability (CTPL) insurance is required for your annual vehicle registration by the Land Transportation Office (LTO).

CTPL pays for expenses incurred as a result of the vehicle owner's causing bodily injury or death to any third party in an accident arising from the use of the insured motor vehicle.

CPI's ECTPL service is the newest and most convinient way to get your CTPL needs. You may avail it online and get your policy as soon as you made your transaction.

Purchase your CTPL policy through us today at the tariff rates set by the Insurance Commission of the Philippines. Upon purchase, we will email the Certificate of Cover (COC) direct to you and you may bring this with you to the LTO for registration of your vehicle.
